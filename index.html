State/District Level Aggregated data Analysis of child malnutrition in Indian Scenario

Our project is in builds visual analytics tools and techniques for understanding the raw data of National Family Health Survey, namely NFHS-4, dataset. NFHS-4 data describes the health indicators of all of India in a snapshot of time, in 2015-16. Our focus has been to perform spatial analysis of triple burden of malnutrition, including the degree of occurrence. The expected outcome is a visual analytics tool to explore different sections of the data available in NFHS-4, which upon generalization can be used for future editions of NFHS.

Here we have choosen one such case study i.e., undernutrition among the triple burden of malnutrition.
